Our Services

At Domain Expansion LTD, we specialize in providing a comprehensive range of architectural services to meet your residential needs. Our expertise includes:

Extension Plans

Transform your existing home with our expertly crafted extension plans. We design seamless additions that enhance the functionality and aesthetic appeal of your property.

New Build Projects

Our new build projects are designed from the ground up to meet your exact specifications. We manage every aspect of the project, ensuring high standards of quality and craftsmanship.

Planning Applications

Navigate the complexities of planning applications with ease. Our team prepares detailed planning work applications to help you secure the necessary approvals for your project.

Construction Drawings

Our construction drawings adhere to building regulations, providing precise and detailed plans to ensure compliance and smooth execution of your construction project.

Our Process

1. Initial Consultation

Our free initial consultation is an informal discussion with you, the homeowner, to understand your vision and the feasibility of the project.

2. Measured Survey

We conduct a measured survey to produce an accurate representation of your property, including floor plans, elevation, basic sections, and site layout.

3. Sketch Designs

We provide several sketch designs to explore different ways to utilize the space, allowing you to mix and match elements to achieve your desired design.

4. Planning Application

If required, we handle the planning application process, ensuring that your project complies with all necessary regulations.

5. Building Regulations

We prepare a detailed building regulation package to ensure compliance with all necessary building standards, providing detailed plans for construction.
